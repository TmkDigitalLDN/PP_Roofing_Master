Pitched Roofs

Providing you with complete peace of mind for the future, Perfect Pitch Roofing offers a comprehensive roofing solution to replace your old roof and install a brand-new, beautiful, and dependable roof.

We work with reputable suppliers and have extensive experience with many different types of home roofs as well as other more challenging tasks. We provide you with a roof of the greatest calibre, one that will last you for many years to come, covered by insurance-backed assurances.

We will also search for ways to enhance your roof's insulation and weatherproofing as part of our full roofing service. We will give you valuable guidance regarding the compatibility of materials using our extensive industry knowledge.

Flat Roofs

We have the knowledge to recommend and install the ideal roofing solution, whether you need a rapid fix for an existing flat roof or you require a fantastic looking, long lasting flat roofing system.

Here are the different types of our services on flat roofs:

Polyurethane Liquid Roofing

High tensile and rip strength single coat application is ideal for covering existing felt.

Liquid Repair Roofing Systems

Instant waterproof protection, adaptable to moist environments, and suitable for a cheap temporary patch.

GRP Fibreglass Roofing

A smooth, modern appearance is provided by durability and an impact-resistant coating. ideal for foot traffic as well.

EPDM Rubber Roofing

Easy to install, durable, low-maintenance, and long-lasting with a life expectancy of up to 50 years.
