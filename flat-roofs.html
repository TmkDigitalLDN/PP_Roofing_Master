Flat Roofs

Flat Roofs

We have the knowledge to recommend and install the ideal roofing solution, whether you need a rapid fix for an existing flat roof or you require a fantastic looking, long lasting flat roofing system. When we do flat roofs we can either remove the old roof and lay on existing boards or we can replace old rotten board with a new installation.

Here are the different types of our services on flat roofs:

Polyurethane Liquid Roofing

High tensile and rip strength single coat application is ideal for covering existing felt.

Three Layer felt system

The three layer felt system consists of a tac-down layer, torched on mid-layer and a torched on cap layer.

GRP Fibreglass Roofing

A smooth, modern appearance is provided by durability and an impact-resistant coating. ideal for foot traffic as well. The GRP Fibreglass system consists of fibre glass roll mat and bandage followed by a resin based coat and then finished with a top coat.

EPDM Rubber Roofing

Easy to install, durable, low-maintenance, and long-lasting with a life expectancy of up to 50 years.
